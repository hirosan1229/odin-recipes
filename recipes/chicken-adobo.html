Chicken Adobo

Description

Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish known for its rich, savory, and slightly tangy flavor. It is made by braising chicken in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns, creating a deep, umami-rich sauce. The slow simmering process allows the chicken to absorb the bold flavors, resulting in tender, juicy meat with a beautifully caramelized exterior. Some variations include adding sugar for a touch of sweetness or coconut milk for a creamy twist, but the traditional balance of salty, sour, and slightly spicy elements remains at the heart of this beloved dish.

This dish is a staple in Filipino households and is often enjoyed with steamed white rice, which soaks up the flavorful sauce. Its simplicity and use of pantry staples make it a go-to comfort food that is easy to prepare yet incredibly satisfying. The longer it sits, the better the flavors meld, making it a great dish for leftovers. Chicken Adobo’s versatility has made it a favorite not just in the Philippines but around the world, as it continues to be a symbol of Filipino culinary heritage.

    Ingredients

    Main Ingredients:

  • 1 kg (2.2 lbs) chicken (thighs, drumsticks, or a mix)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce (preferably Filipino brands like Silver Swan or Datu Puti)
  • 1/3 cup vinegar (white vinegar or cane vinegar)
  • 5 cloves garlic (minced or crushed)
  • 1 small onion (sliced, optional)
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 2-3 dried bay leaves
  • 1 cup water (or chicken broth for extra flavor)

    • Optional Ingredients(for variations)

  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (for a slightly sweet taste)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (for a creamy version)>
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (for added umami)
  • 1 tsp paprika (for a smoky touch)
  • 2 red chilies (for a spicy kick)
  • 1 small potato or boiled eggs (added for extra texture)

    • For Garnish & Serving

  • Chopped green onions or parsley (optional)
  • Steamed white rice (for serving)

    Step-by-step Cooking Guide

  1. Marinate the Chicken (Optional but Recommended)
  2. Sear the Chicken
  3. Sauté Aromatics
  4. Simmer the Chicken in Sauce
  5. Adjust Flavor & Add Extras (Optional)
  6. Reduce Sauce & Serve
  7. Serve & Enjoy
